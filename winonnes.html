WHAT THE FUCK WHERE YOU THINKING !! Windows on Nintendo Entertainment System!? Why? Who asked for this?
Well... In the case of both Russian and Chinese Windows 2000 I think I may have found out:
The famiclone manufacturers asked for it. In case you never heard of it "famiclones" are illegal reverse engineered Famicoms (What they called the Nintendo in Japan).
While most famiclones just had a cartridge slot and controllers some of them pretended to be computers!
They where designed like 80's computers with the computer and keyboard being one unit.
Others however would have the keyboard separate. Most of them would also feature 3.5" floppy disks and PS/2 ports and other connectors. Hell you could even get a printer !!
However... This was not enough. After all... All computers have a operating system so they needed one too...
So yeah, that's probably the reason for Windows 2000. It would probably boot from a cartridge or be stored in internal hardware.
An interesting thing to point out is that there where several other operating systems for famiclones some had a simple GUI that usually ripped off Windows
while others would be pretty advanced DOS operating systems. Some of these famiclone operating systems would even have programming tools.
Sounds interesting? Well that's not all, some of these systems would boot from a 3.5" floppy disk !!

I'm not sure what windows 98 for NES was intended for but i doubt that it could have been a famiclone os.
The two variants of Windows 2000 had several features, programs and games that where standard on keyboard famiclones.
And a lot of software and games in them needs a keyboard.
Windows 98 on the other hand has none of that. Hell, it doesn't have any usable programs at all,
the programs are just images of programs. (No literally they are just images of em').
